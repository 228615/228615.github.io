About Bobatron

Bobatron is a rock band from Los Angeles, California. The band was formed in 2010 by lead vocalist and guitarist Bob, who was later joined by Alice on guitar, Charlie on bass, and Dave on drums. Since its inception, Bobatron has released three studio albums and toured extensively across the United States and Europe.

Bobatron's music is influenced by classic rock bands such as Led Zeppelin and Black Sabbath, as well as more modern acts like the Arctic Monkeys and the Strokes. The band's sound is characterized by catchy riffs, powerful vocals, and energetic live performances.

Meet the band

Bob

Bob is the lead vocalist and guitarist for Bobatron. He started playing music at a young age and has always had a passion for rock and roll. In addition to his work with the band, Bob is also a skilled songwriter and producer.

Alice

Alice is the lead guitarist for Bobatron. She has been playing guitar since she was a teenager and is known for her technical proficiency and creative solos. Alice is also a talented backing vocalist and contributes to the band's songwriting process.

Charlie

Charlie is the bassist for Bobatron. He has a background in jazz and funk music and brings a groovy, upbeat feel to the band's sound. Charlie is also an accomplished music producer and has worked on several of the band's albums.

Dave

Dave is the drummer for Bobatron. He has been playing drums since he was a child and is known for his precise timing and powerful style. Dave is also an experienced live performer and helps keep the energy high during Bobatron's concerts.
